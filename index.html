Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

Pathora ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the Pathora numerology app.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information that you provide directly:

• Full birth name

• Date of birth

• Saved profile readings (name and birth date combinations)

• Subscription plan preference (Basic, Monthly, or Yearly)

This information is used solely to generate your numerology readings and to provide the app's features.

3. How We Store Your Data

All data you enter is stored locally on your device using secure storage (AsyncStorage). We do not transmit your name, birth date, or profile data to our servers or any third-party servers. Your information never leaves your device except as described in Section 4.

4. Third-Party Services

The Horoscope feature fetches daily horoscope content from a third-party API (freehoroscopeapi.com). When you use this feature, we send only your zodiac sign (derived from your birth date) to retrieve horoscope text. No personally identifiable information (name or full birth date) is sent to this service.

5. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

• Generate numerology calculations and readings

• Save and display your profile readings

• Provide horoscope content based on your zodiac sign

• Remember your subscription plan preference

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

6. Data Retention

Your data remains on your device until you delete it. You can delete individual saved profiles or all profiles at any time from the Saved Readings screen. Uninstalling the app will remove all stored data from your device.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

• Access the data we store (it is visible within the app)

• Delete your data at any time (use "Delete all" in Saved Readings)

• Stop using the app and uninstall it to remove all data

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA) or California, you may have additional rights under GDPR or CCPA. Contact us to exercise these rights.

8. Children's Privacy

Pathora is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Security

We store your data locally on your device. We recommend that you keep your device secure (e.g., use a passcode or biometric lock) to protect your information.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date. Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us at:

support@pathora-numbers.com

You can also find our contact information in the app store listing.
